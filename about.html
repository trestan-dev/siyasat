About SiyasatPH

Transparency and Safety in the Philippine Digital Landscape.

OUR MISSION

SiyasatPH is a dedicated public safety initiative designed to provide Filipinos with an offline-first, real-time repository of verified enforcement actions. We aim to bridge the gap between regulatory transparency and public awareness by making "Cease and Desist" orders and advisory bulletins accessible to everyone, anywhere — even without internet access.

HOW WE COLLECT INFO

Our data is synchronized with the latest official releases from the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC), the Cybercrime Investigation and Coordinating Center (CICC), and the National Privacy Commission (NPC). We focus on identifying patterns of Ponzi schemes, illegal lending apps, and task-based scams affecting our local community.

A COLLECTIVE DEFENSE

By using the Risk Audit tool, users contribute to a growing database of digital threats. While we are an AI-assisted portal, our priority remains grounded in human safety and the protection of Filipino hard-earned assets.

DISCLAIMER

SiyasatPH is an independent public safety project and is not affiliated with any government agency. All enforcement data referenced herein is sourced from publicly available official government advisories. This portal is for informational and advisory purposes only. Always verify information through official government channels before taking any legal or financial action.
